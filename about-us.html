Meet the guys behind Major Cleans

Our Beginning

Major Cleans was founded by John, a lad with a passion for cars. Working as a mechanic from a young age he always wanted to work with cars. Have noticed the lack of mobile valet providers in the Liverpool area Major Cleans was born. Having had a successful first year running Major Cleans John decided it was time to grow, a couple of beers and long disscussion later he had enlisted life long friend Ste to come on board and run the business together!

What we are up to now

Over the last six months John and Ste have been running the business together, while both working full-time, to get make Major Cleans a success. Using social media as a platform to promote and sell the offering reaching people all over the north west. Check out our socials below to see our leastest before and after photos. And feel free to ask for a quote or any special requests.

Our Future

Having recently revamped the services for our customers we plan to run a quarterly social media competition for customers to win free showroom valets every three months.
